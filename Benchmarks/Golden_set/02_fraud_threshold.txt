Veridia Billing Policy — Fraud Detection Update

Effective this release, the automated fraud-detection threshold for new payment methods has been lowered, meaning transactions that previously passed automatically now require additional verification more often.

This adjustment was made in response to a rise in chargeback rates reported by our payment processor over the last two quarters.

The billing team has also updated the customer-facing FAQ to clarify typical processing times for new payment methods, though the FAQ language has not yet been updated to reflect the most recent threshold change.

Because more transactions now require manual verification, the average time to clear a payout for affected accounts has increased from same-day to 2-3 business days, and the manual review queue has grown accordingly.

The team is evaluating whether additional review staff are needed to keep pace with the increased queue volume.
