Veridia Billing Policy — Payment Processing

When a scheduled payment fails, the system automatically retries the charge up to 3 times over 7 days. If all 3 retry attempts fail, the account is suspended and access to paid features is revoked.

Customers are notified by email after each failed attempt, giving them the opportunity to update their payment method before suspension occurs.

Suspended accounts can be reactivated immediately once a valid payment method is provided.
