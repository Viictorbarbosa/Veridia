Veridia Billing Policy — Refund Handling

Refunds are typically issued when customers report a billing issue within 14 days of the charge. Our support team reviews each case individually, taking into account account history and the nature of the issue reported.

In some cases, refunds may also be granted outside the 14-day window at the discretion of the billing team, particularly for enterprise accounts with dedicated account managers.

Refund requests submitted through the self-service portal are processed faster than those submitted via email, though both follow the same underlying review process.
