Veridia Master Services Agreement — Data Handling Addendum

Client data is retained for a period of 90 days following contract termination, to allow for account recovery in case of accidental cancellation or disputes over termination terms.

During this retention period, data is stored in an access-restricted archive tier and is not available through the standard production API.

Separately, this addendum was updated to comply with recent regional data-protection requirements affecting how deletion requests are handled across all active and terminated accounts.

Because terminated-account data now remains recoverable for the full 90-day retention window described above, any deletion request received during that window is processed as a soft delete with a 90-day grace period, rather than immediate permanent erasure, unless the client explicitly requests immediate permanent deletion in writing.
